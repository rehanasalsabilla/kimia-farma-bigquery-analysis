# 📊 Dashboard Explanation - Kimia Farma

This dashboard is designed to analyze Kimia Farma’s business performance from 2020 to 2023 using transaction, branch, and product data.

---

## 🔝 Key Metrics (KPI)
- **Total Net Sales**: Total revenue after discounts
- **Total Net Profit**: Total profit generated
- **Total Transactions**: Number of transactions
- **Average Rating**: Average transaction rating
- **Profit Margin**: Profitability ratio (profit vs sales)

👉 These KPIs provide an overall view of business performance.

---

## 📈 Sales Trend
Displays Net Sales trends from 2020 to 2023.

👉 Purpose:
- Identify business growth or decline
- Understand yearly performance patterns

---

## 🏪 Top Branch Performance
Includes:
- Top Branch by Total Transactions
- Top Branch by Net Sales

👉 Purpose:
- Identify branches with the highest activity (volume)
- Identify branches generating the highest revenue (value)

---

## 🗺️ Profit Distribution by Province
Shows profit distribution across provinces.

👉 Purpose:
- Identify regions contributing the most profit
- Understand business concentration and imbalance

---

## ⭐ Rating Analysis
Compares branch ratings with transaction ratings.

👉 Purpose:
- Identify gaps between perceived branch quality and actual customer experience
- Detect potential operational issues

---

## 💰 Discount Analysis
Shows the impact of discount levels on average profit.

👉 Purpose:
- Analyze the relationship between discount and profitability
- Evaluate the effectiveness of discount strategies

---

## 🎯 Conclusion
This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance, including sales trends, branch performance, geographic distribution, customer experience, and profitability drivers.
