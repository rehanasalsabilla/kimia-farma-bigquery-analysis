# 📊 Business Insights - Kimia Farma

## 1. Sales Performance Trend
Net Sales from 2020 to 2023 show a relatively stable trend with no significant growth over time. Although there are slight fluctuations between years, the overall performance indicates that the business is stable but not expanding.

## 2. Branch Performance
Branches in West Java dominate both transaction volume and total sales compared to other regions. This indicates that business performance is still concentrated in specific branches and has not been evenly distributed across all regions.

## 3. Geographical Distribution
Most of the profit is generated from West Java, with a significant gap compared to other provinces. This highlights a strong dependency on a single region, which may pose a business risk.

## 4. Customer Experience (Rating)
There is a noticeable gap between high branch ratings and lower transaction ratings. This suggests potential issues in service quality or operational processes that negatively affect the customer experience.

## 5. Discount Impact on Profit
Higher discount levels are associated with lower profit. This indicates that the current discount strategy is not optimal and may reduce overall profitability.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Develop growth strategies to improve sales performance that is currently stagnant.
2. Reduce dependency on specific regions by improving branch performance in other provinces.
3. Optimize discount strategies to balance sales growth and profitability.
4. Improve service quality and operational processes to enhance customer experience.

---

# 🏁 Conclusion

Kimia Farma’s business performance is stable but lacks significant growth. The business is highly dependent on certain regions and faces operational challenges that impact profitability. Strategic improvements are needed to drive growth and improve overall efficiency.
